Meet Eye Hope Canada.

Eye Hope Canada (EHC) is a student-led initiative based in Toronto, Canada. Our goal is to raise awareness about challenges facing the childhood eye cancer community and mobilise the public to solve them. Co-directing EHC, alongside my sister, has been a privilege as I've met many resilient cancer survivors and patients, inspiring healthcare professionals, and passionate student volunteers.

So, what have we been up to recently?

Hover to learn more about my role in each project! To learn more about the impact of each project, please visit our website (eyehopecanada.com).


Order-In Charity Concert

We're currently in the works of organizing a virtual charity concert featuring amazing performers, inspirational speakers, and over $1000 in prizes.

My Role:

  • Ideated event idea and hosted team brainstorming meetings
  • Contacted performers (ie. singers, dancers, spoken word artists)
  • Secured $1000 in sponsorships and a $350 grant

Resilience

Resilience is a collection of poetry, short stories, and art that depict the stories of 14 cancer survivors, patients, and carers.

My Role:

  • Marketed book on social media, reaching 600 people
  • Collected $500 of revenue in 2 weeks

Mural Project @ Aaniin Community Centre

We partnered with elementary schools across Markham to create a 15-piece mural that symbolizes community, survivorship, and Markham's collective support for the childhood cancer community.

My Role:

  • Oversaw partnerships with elementary schools and sponsorships
  • Organized partnership with the City of Markham
  • Spearheaded an online-version of the mural campaign (due to the postponement of the mural's installation during COVID-19)

Awareness

We use our social media platform (@eyehopecanada) to raise awareness about issues specific to our cause. Our #ArtistsofInstagram campaign also featured community artwork about survivorship!

My Role:

  • Prepared easy-to-access marketing schedules for designers and social media coordinators
  • Edited and proofread informational posts

Educational Panels

Our panels range from a variety of topics including research and careers in healthcare. We had the opportunity to work with doctors, global health researchers, and cancer survivors to make these events possible!

My Role:

  • Developed event itinerary
  • Contacted speakers
