reimagining it

why redesign Chatime Societea?

As an avid bubble tea drinker, I use the Chatime Societea app quite a bit. However, when I downloaded it, it took me 10 minutes to figure out how to collect points (let alone all of the other features). As a result, I decided to combine my passion for boba and UX design in order to redesign the Chatime Societea app. Since my first project was an original app design I created, I wanted to try something different--hence, this project!

the app

what is Chatime Societea?

Chatime is a bubble tea franchise that is well-established across the globe. Currently, they have an app called Chatime Societea where users can digitally collect points upon making an in-store purchase. They also have a separate website called Chatime Online Ordering where users can order their bubble tea online in-advance and then pick it up.


After conducting user research, I identitfied 4 main problems: separate point collection and ordering feature, disrupting the workflow of Chatime employees, confusing user flow and design, and unclear instructions. In my case study, I explore all of these issues and share how I solved them!

reflection

here's what i learned.

This was my second time designing an app. As a result, I had a lot of fidnings from my first app that I could apply to this one. I had trouble finding my own design as I was constantly worried that my design would not match the style of the old Chatime Societea app. However, I realized that as a designer, I should express myself and how I interpret certain things. From there, I felt more comfortable experimenting with new styles and layouts.


Skills I Improved On: User/ Market Research, User Personas & Flows, Wireframing (Balsamiq), Low & High-Fidelity Prototyping (Figma), Usability Scripts & Tests, Style Guides, Crtical Thinking
